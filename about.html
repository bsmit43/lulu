About Lulu Accounting

Our Story

Lulu Accounting was founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson, a Certified Public Accountant with over 15 years of experience in the financial industry. Sarah envisioned a firm that could provide personalized, accessible, and reliable accounting services to small and medium-sized businesses. Starting as a small operation in her home office, Lulu Accounting has grown into a trusted partner for businesses across various sectors.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and helping them achieve their financial goals. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to providing accurate, timely, and insightful financial advice.
